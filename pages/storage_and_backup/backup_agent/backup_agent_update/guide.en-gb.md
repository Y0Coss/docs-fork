## Objective

You have the Backup Agent product and you want to understand how your agents will be updated.

## Requirements

* You must have the Backup Agent product.
* At least one bare metal server with an agent installed and configured.

## In practice

Updates are offered periodically by Veeam.

As a service provider, we will ensure that we offer updates as soon as they are available.

To do this, we will send you communications by email to inform you that an update must be made, and that it will be done on a certain date, and time slot.

In practice, when the D-Day update is triggered, your agent will be reinstalled on your operating system silently, without any necessary action on your part.

During this update, backup and restore operations will be unavailable. As a result, we will make them over the period of the CET and EST day depending on your location in order to avoid interfering with your automatic backups.

If you have any specific requests, please feel free to contact OVHcloud support.
